ABOUT US

GOLDENCET FARMER'S PRODUCER COMPANY LIMITED

Goldencet farmers producer organisation is based in the golden city of rajasthan india "jaisalmer" which is also known as city of "sand dunes" or thar desert city. The reason behind the incorporation of this FPO is "from the farmers,for the farmers and to the farmers." We have huge registered farmer team of 2000 and their major focus crops are cumin and groundnuts.
